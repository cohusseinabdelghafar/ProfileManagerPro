Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 9, 2025

Thank you for choosing Profile Manager Pro ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our browser extension.

1. Information We Collect

Profile Manager Pro is designed to help you manage multiple browser profiles with minimal data collection. We collect:

  • Browser Profile Data: Information about your browser profiles is stored locally on your device to enable the functionality of the extension.
  • Cookie Data: The extension requires access to browser cookies to properly manage and switch between profiles.
  • Storage Data: We use browser storage to save your profile configurations and preferences.

All data collected is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Enable the core functionality of the Profile Manager Pro extension
  • Save your profile settings and preferences
  • Provide a seamless profile switching experience

3. Data Storage and Security

All data collected by Profile Manager Pro is stored locally on your device using browser storage mechanisms. We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

4. Third-Party Services

Profile Manager Pro does not share your data with any third-party services or external servers. All operations are performed locally on your device.

5. Your Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can access all data stored by the extension in your browser's local storage.
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the extension will remove all associated data from your device.
  • Control: You can modify your profiles and settings at any time through the extension interface.

6. Children's Privacy

Profile Manager Pro is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@profilemanager.pro
