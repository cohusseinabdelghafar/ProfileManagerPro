Frequently Asked Questions

What is Profile Manager Pro?

Profile Manager Pro is a free browser extension that helps you manage and switch between multiple browser profiles with just one click.

How do I install it?

Simply click the "Add to Chrome" or "Add to Firefox" button above and follow the browser's installation prompts.

Is it really free?

Yes! Profile Manager Pro is completely free to use with all features included.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely! We prioritize your privacy and security. The extension operates locally on your browser and doesn't collect any personal data.
